Company History


Our company has been doing business in and around Dane County, Wisconsin, since 2002. We are dedicated to meeting your needs. To accomplish this goal, our properties are maintained at the highest standard possible.

About Our Properties


Our duplexes are located in Cottage Grove, Wisconsin, and offer the benefits of a small town with the covenience of the metropolitan culture of Madison. Our properties are conveniently located within minutes to I-90 and Highways 12 & 18. We are approximately 15 minutes to the Dane County International Airport and East Towne Mall. Grocery, banking, and post office are also only minutes away.

What we look for in a tenant

Understanding what we look for in a renter will help you in all stages of the application process.


smoke_free

Smoke Free

All our properties are Smoke Free

home

Good Rental History

A record of paying rent on time, not causing disturbances, damages, or violating rental rules. Five years rental history must be provided. We require satisfactory references from at least one prior and current landlord or an equivalent from first-time renters

attach_money

Income Sufficient to Manage the Rent

The industry standard is a combined gross monthly income from all applicants to be equal to or greater than three times the monthly rent after expenses. (A unit renting for $1,000 per month requires a suggested income of $3,000 per month)



credit_card

Credit History

Bad credit history or a lack of can be a legal reason for denying an application. Your credit history includes credit cards, bank accounts, and loans. We prefer a credit score of 600 or higher

no_encryption

Criminal History

We do not accept applications from individuals who have been convicted for an offense that would "cause a reasonable person to have a justifiable fear for their safety"

pets

Pets

We accept pets on a case-by-case basis with an additional monthly fee. However, our insurance company prohibits us from renting to individuals with Akitas, Chows, Pit Bulls a.k.a. Staffordshire Terriers, Presa Canarias, Rottweilers, Sharpeis, or Wolf Hybrids
